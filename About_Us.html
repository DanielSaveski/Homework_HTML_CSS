Welcome to Chop Chop, Macedonia's premier axe-throwing venue! Founded by two brothers, our passion for this thrilling sport began during our travels around the world. We've experienced the adrenaline rush of axe throwing in various countries and were inspired to bring this exciting and unique activity back home.

Our Story

Growing up, we always had a love for adventure and trying new things. On one of our trips abroad, we stumbled upon an axe-throwing bar and were instantly hooked. The combination of skill, precision, and sheer fun captivated us. We knew then that this was something special, something we wanted to share with others. After countless games and a deep dive into the art and safety of axe throwing, we decided to turn our passion into a reality. Chop Chop was born out of our desire to create a space where people could come together, challenge themselves, and most importantly, have an unforgettable time.

What We Offer

At Chop Chop, we offer an exhilarating and unique entertainment experience for adults. Whether you're looking to test your skills, blow off some steam, or just have a great time with friends, our state-of-the-art axe throwing lanes and interactive games are designed to provide endless fun and excitement.
